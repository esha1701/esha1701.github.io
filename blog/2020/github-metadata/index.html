Health Science & Well-being Podcasts That Changed My Life

September 28, 2021

2021

The Peter Attia Drive Podcast hosted by Dr Peter Attia, a Stanford/Johns Hopkins trained physician, The Drive is a podcast on the applied science of longevity and maximising human healthspan.
Episodes I love: The Pervasive Effect of Stress, Dangers of Poor Sleep, How to Thrive with Type 1 Diabetes


Huberman Lab Podcast hosted by Andrew Huberman, a professor of neurobiology and ophthalmology at Stanford University School of Medicine, discusses how neuroscience controls our perception, behaviour and health.
Episodes I love: How Your Brain Works & Changes, The Science of Emotions & Relationships


The Matt Walker Podcast Matthew Walker, Professor of Neuroscience and Psychology at the University of California Berkeley, talks about the science of sleep and its impact on health.
Episodes I love: Circadian Rhythms


Lex Fridman Podcast Lex Fridman, an AI researcher at MIT discusses the nature of intelligence, consciousness, love, and power.
Episodes I love: : Consciousness, Free Will, AI, and Meaning, Kernel Brain-Computer Interfaces
